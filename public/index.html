b-wordle instructions

Guess the word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. If the letter is GREEN, it is in in the word and the right position. If the letter is ORANGE, it is in the word but in the wrong position. If the letter is GREY, it is not in the word. A new word will be available each day.
